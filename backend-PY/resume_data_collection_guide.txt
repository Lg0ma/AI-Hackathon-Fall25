Before you start filling out your resume, make sure you have:

[ ] Your complete contact information (phone number required, email optional)

TIPS FOR SUCCESS:
- Use numbers whenever possible (managed 5 people, moved 200 pallets daily)
- Focus on what you ACHIEVED, not just what you were supposed to do
- Only list skills and certifications you actually have
- Keep certifications current - expired ones don't count
- Proofread everything - spelling and grammar matter!
